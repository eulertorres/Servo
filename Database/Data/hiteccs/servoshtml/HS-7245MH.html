HS-7245MH

16.8mm, Coreless, High Voltage, High Torque, Metal Gear Actuator (7.4V)
 

Our high voltage, coreless HS-7245MH servo delivers exceptional torque in a compact package. Built with an efficient coreless motor optimized for 7.4V operation and heavy-duty metal gear train with dual ball bearings, this high-end actuator delivers unbridled performance for applications requiring general-use, precision-control actuation.  With programmable functions including end point adjustments, fail safe, overload protection and data load, to name a few and G2.5 fully programmable digital circuitry, the HS-7245MH is the gold standard in a torque-driven mini servo.
